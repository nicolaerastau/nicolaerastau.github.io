FC Bayern Munich

Everybody that watches football nowadays knows who and what FC Bayern Munich is. FC Bayern Munich is a football team from München, Germany. The football team is playing in the Bundesliga, one of the most competitive league in the whole world. In the past few years Bayern Munich has been on top of all teams in European, North and South American football and even Asia. The team was founded on 27 February 1900, 115 years ago and ever since the team won 25 national titles, 17 national cups, 5 UEFA Champions League and once the UEFA Super Cup. These are some great achievements, not many teams have so many leagues and titles won. Home ground for FC Bayern Munich is the one and only Allianz Arena with a capacity of 75,000 supporters. As I said before Bundesliga is one of the most competitive leagues and that means that Bayern Munich have many rivals. Their biggest rival at the moment is Borussia Dortmund. Josep “Pep” Guardiola Sala is the coach of FC Bayern Munich since 2013. People seem to like him since the team is doing great, but there are always people that will think otherwise.

FC Real Madrid

Real Madrid is one of the two big Spanish football teams. Real Madrid is known for its rivalry with FC Barcelona, the other big Spanish football team. The club is based in Madrid the capital of Spain and it was founded in 1902 as Madrid Football Club. In 1920 King Alfonso XIII gave the club the title of “Royal” which in Spanish is “Real”. FC Real Madrid won La Liga 32 times, Copa del Rey 19 times, UEFA Champions League 10 times and UEFA Super Cup 4 times. Real is the only club that won the European Cup five years in a row. Rafael Benítez is the current coach of the club. He has not been Real’s coach for a long time, but the results are really promising. The home ground for Real Madrid is Santiago Bernabeú Stadium and it is huge. 85 044 supporters can watch the games.
